The Love for Welding

Welding is a process that joins metals using heat, pressure, or electricity. It is widely used in construction, automotive, and manufacturing industries.

TIG (Tungsten Inert Gas)

TIG welding is a precise method using a tungsten electrode and shielding gas.

MIG (Metal Inert Gas)

MIG welding uses a wire electrode and shielding gas to join metal.
